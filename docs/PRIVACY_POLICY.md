# PropBox IQ — Privacy Policy

_Last updated: May 17, 2026_

PropBox IQ ("we," "us," "our") provides a real estate deal analysis app and
web service at propboxiq.com and on the App Store. This policy explains
what data we collect, why, and what we do with it.

If you have any questions, email **info@propboxiq.com**.

## What we collect

We collect only what's needed to run the service:

- **Email address.** Used to sign you in via magic link and to send you
  service notifications (e.g. password-reset-equivalent flows). We do not use
  your email for marketing without your explicit opt-in.
- **Deals you save.** When you analyze a property and save it, we store the
  address, purchase price, rehab budget, ARV, and the resulting calculations
  in your account.
- **Server logs.** Standard HTTP request logs (timestamp, route, status) for
  debugging and abuse prevention. These are kept for 30 days.

## What we don't collect

- We do not collect your name (unless you provide it on a deal).
- We do not collect your phone number, location, photos, contacts, or
  microphone/camera data.
- We do not embed third-party trackers, advertising SDKs, or analytics SDKs
  that profile users across apps and websites.
- We do not sell or rent your data to anyone, ever.

## How we use third-party services

PropBox IQ relies on a small number of vendors to provide market data:

- **ATTOM Data** and **RentCast** — we query these providers for property
  records, comparable sales, and ZIP-code market statistics. We send only the
  property address or ZIP code; we do not send your email or personal data.
- **Render** — our cloud hosting provider. Your account data and saved deals
  are stored on Render's infrastructure in the United States.
- **Resend** (or equivalent transactional email provider) — used solely to
  deliver magic-link sign-in emails.

## Data retention

- Your account and deals are retained as long as your account exists.
- You can request account deletion at any time by emailing
  info@propboxiq.com. We delete the account and associated deals within 30
  days of the request.
- After deletion, anonymized aggregate usage (e.g. "X deals analyzed in
  ZIP 21146 last month") may persist in our analytics.

## Children

PropBox IQ is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly
collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has provided us
data, contact info@propboxiq.com.

## Security

- All traffic uses HTTPS / TLS.
- Session cookies are HttpOnly, Secure, and rotate every 30 days.
- Production secrets (API keys for ATTOM, RentCast, etc.) are stored in
  Render's encrypted environment variables — not in source code.

## Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. California under CCPA, EU under GDPR), you
have the right to:

- Request a copy of the data we hold about you.
- Request correction of inaccurate data.
- Request deletion of your data.
- Opt out of any non-essential email.

To exercise any of these, email **info@propboxiq.com** and we'll respond
within 30 days.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be
posted here with a new "Last updated" date and, where appropriate,
announced in the app.
